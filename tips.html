Cooking Tips

Keep it simple

Use few ingredients at first, don’t stress. Simple meals are still tasty.

Prep before you cook

Chop and measure ingredients before starting. Makes cooking faster and less messy.

Use fresh ingredients

Fresh veggies and fruits make your food taste better. Frozen is ok too sometimes.

Don’t overcrowd the pan

Cook in batches if needed. Food cooks better and gets a nice color.

Taste as you go

Add salt or seasoning slowly and taste often. Helps avoid over-salting.
